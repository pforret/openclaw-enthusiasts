    OpenClaw Use Cases¶

    Discover how people use OpenClaw to automate real-world tasks — from email triage and server monitoring to content pipelines and smart home control. Each post is a step-by-step guide you can follow.

    Weekly YouTube and Substack Analytics Reports

    Get a weekly performance digest for your creator channels -- subscriber growth, top-performing content, engagement trends, and actionable suggestions -- delivered straight to Telegram.
    Feb 27, 2026

    Voice Note to Journal Transcription

    Send a voice memo to WhatsApp or Telegram and OpenClaw transcribes it, cleans it up, extracts action items, and saves a structured journal entry to Notion.
    Feb 26, 2026

    Self-Healing Server Monitor

    An OpenClaw agent that checks your servers, diagnoses common failures, applies known fixes automatically, and escalates to you only when it can't resolve the issue itself.
    Feb 25, 2026

    Competitor Price Monitoring

    Track competitor pricing across e-commerce sites and get instant alerts when prices change -- so you can adjust your own pricing before customers notice.
    Feb 24, 2026

    Email Inbox Triage with Smart Sorting

    Let OpenClaw read your inbox, sort messages by urgency, draft replies to routine emails, and auto-unsubscribe from newsletters you never open.
    Feb 24, 2026

    AI Video Story Editor: 500GB of Footage to 16 Films

    OpenClaw orchestrates Gemini, Mistral, and Claude to turn raw travel footage into story-edited films with narrative arcs, editor notes, and FCPXML timelines -- ready for Final Cut Pro or DaVinci Resolve.

    Overnight SEO Blog Writer

    Set up an OpenClaw agent that researches keywords, writes SEO-optimized blog posts, and queues them in your CMS while you sleep -- wake up to fresh content ready for review.
